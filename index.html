Hi, I’m Kelvin van Hoorn, an experienced technical artist that loves to make stylised art through shaders, VFX and programming.

After finishing my Applied Physics Master I decided I wanted to work in the games industry, specfically on the graphical art side. In my spare time I taught myself to write shaders through online tutorials, experimentation and art challenges.

Since then I’ve worked at Pixel Toys on a yet unnanounced game as an experienced technical artist. There my resposibilities include shaders, render features, VFX, UI, tools, and optimisng the game the achieve its performance targets.
